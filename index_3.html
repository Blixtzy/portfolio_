About Me

Building Worlds
From Scratch

I'm Sainath - a 3D artist who transforms raw ideas into breathtaking environments, sculpted models, and cinematic animations. Every scene tells a story through geometry, light, and texture.

My pipeline: Blender and V-Ray for modeling and rendering, ZBrush for sculpting, RealityCapture for photogrammetry, and Unreal Engine for real-time world-building.

Post-production handled with Photoshop for texturing and Premiere Pro plus After Effects for polished cinematic outputs.

Currently available for freelance and collaboration.
